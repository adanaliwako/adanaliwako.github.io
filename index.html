Our Ecosystem

At Skyfield, we harness the power of cutting-edge drone technology to transform farming. Our autonomous spraying drones deliver fast, efficient, and eco-friendly solutions for crop spraying, pest control, and field monitoring—saving farmers time, reducing costs, and boosting yields. Whether you're managing a small plot or vast acres of land, Skyfield Drone Services brings precision, innovation, and sustainability right to your farm.

Tujue Ukulima is your go-to digital hub for practical, beginner-friendly farming knowledge. Skyfield Soko is a smart, farmer-powered marketplace revolutionizing how agricultural products are bought and sold. Designed with both the farmer and buyer in mind, our platform connects producers directly to markets, allowing real-time bidding, product showcasing, and verified sales—all from the comfort of your phone. Whether you're selling fresh harvests or sourcing quality produce, Skyfield Soko empowers you to trade smarter, faster, and fairer.
