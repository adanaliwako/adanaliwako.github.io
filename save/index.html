Welcome to Skyfield

Skyfield Aerotech revolutionizes agriculture with advanced drone technology, providing precision spraying and real-time crop monitoring. Our solutions enhance productivity, reduce waste, and promote sustainable farming practices, ensuring farmers achieve optimal yields with minimal environmental impact.

Our Services

Crop Spraying

Our drone-based spraying services provide precise, fast, and cost-effective application of pesticides, fertilizers, and herbicides. Using GPS-guided technology, we ensure accurate delivery of chemicals, reducing waste and environmental impact.

Crop Monitoring

Real-time crop health monitoring using drones equipped with multispectral and thermal sensors. Early detection of issues helps you take proactive measures to improve crop performance.

Fertilizer Application

Precise fertilizer application tailored to crop needs, promoting optimal growth and maximizing yield potential.
